Instant Russian Tea Recipe

A warm, citrusy spice blend that turns a simple cup into a cozy ritual. 🍊☕✨

This instant Russian tea mix is a nostalgic blend of citrus, spice, and gentle sweetness that feels like wrapping your hands around a warm memory. Made with tangy orange notes, hints of cinnamon, and a cozy tea base, it transforms a simple cup of hot water into something comforting and aromatic. Perfect for chilly evenings or slow mornings, this mix also makes a thoughtful homemade gift.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups orange-flavor drink powder
  • 3 ounces lemonade-flavor drink powder
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup instant tea powder
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon ground allspice
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cloves

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine both drink powders in a large bowl.
  3. Mix in sugar, tea powder, cinnamon, allspice, and cloves until well combined.
  4. Divide mixture among three pint-sized jars.
  5. Write the following instructions on three gift tags: "Place 2 to 3 rounded teaspoonfuls into a cup, add boiling water, and serve."
  6. Seal the jars and attach the gift tags.
