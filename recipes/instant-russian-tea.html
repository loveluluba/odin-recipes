Instant Russian Tea Recipe

Description: This instant Russian tea mix can be given as a gift in a jar. It tastes like mulled cider.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups orange-flavor drink powder
  • 3 ounces lemonade-flavor drink powder
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup instant tea powder
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon ground allspice
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cloves

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine both drink powders in a large bowl.
  3. Mix in sugar, tea powder, cinnamon, allspice, and cloves until well combined.
  4. Divide mixture among three pint-sized jars.
  5. Write the following instructions on three gift tags: "Place 2 to 3 rounded teaspoonfuls into a cup, add boiling water, and serve."
  6. Seal the jars and attach the gift tags.
